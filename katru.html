MOVIE KAATRUVELIYIDAI
YEAR 2017

STORY

Tamil romantic drama that intertwines themes of love, war, and personal sacrifice. The film is set against the backdrop of the Kargil War in the late 1990s and follows the story of Varun (played by Karthi), a fighter pilot, and Leela (played by Aditi Rao Hydari), a doctor.

The plot revolves around Varun's tumultuous love affair with Leela, which begins when he is shot down in enemy territory and captured. The narrative explores their relationship, marked by intense emotions, misunderstandings, and the impact of war on personal lives. Leela’s character is portrayed as strong and independent, while Varun grapples with his feelings and the consequences of his actions.

The film features beautiful cinematography, a poignant musical score by A.R. Rahman, and explores deeper themes of loyalty, love, and the complexities of human emotions in the face of conflict. The title translates to "the open sky," symbolizing freedom and the vastness of emotions experienced by the characters.
